Vietnam Trip Guide

Where to Stay

Hanoi

  • Tay Ho (West Lake): Quieter, popular with expats. Everyone seems to recommend staying here for a first time visit to Hanoi.
  • Hoan Kiem (Old Quarter): Busy and tourist-friendly.
  • Ba Dinh: Close to landmarks, less crowded.

Accommodations:

  • Old Quarter View Hanoi Hostel - $16/bed night
  • La Siesta on Ma May Street - $100/night

Da Nang

Vietnam's 5th largest city with a major port.

Attractions:

  • Marble Mountains
  • Golden Bridge
  • Lady Buddha

Accommodations:

  • La Siesta Hoi An Resort and Spa

Itinerary Highlights

  • Hanoi (2 Days): Visit landmarks.
  • Ha Long Bay (1 Day): Bus trip from Hanoi.
  • Hue (1 Day): Explore historical sites.
  • Hoi An/Da Nang (2 Days): Visit beaches and cultural sites.
  • Da Lat (2 Days): Cooler weather and nature.
  • Ho Chi Minh City (3 Days): Explore the city.

Additional Notes

Plan transportation. Bus and domestic flights are common.

Prepare for cooler weather in Hanoi and Da Lat, and warmer weather in Ho Chi Minh City.
