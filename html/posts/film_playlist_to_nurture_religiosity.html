To Nurture Religiosity

Here is an ordered list of movies that contend with religion or dogma and not just spirituality. See this list on letterboxd.

  • The Master (2012) - Freddie finds religion.
  • Life of Pi (2012) - God casts Pi to the desert.
  • Monty Python and the Holy Grail (1975) - Knights of the Round Table quest for holy objects.
  • Andrei Rublev (1966) - Andrei's faith is tested.
  • Life of Brian (1979) - Brian is a normal person.
  • The Mission (1986) - A Jesuit defends the land of the mission field.
  • The Prince of Egypt (1998) - Moses and Ramses are of the same blood and clash.
  • Silence (2016) - Two Jesuit priests struggle in Japan.
  • The Exorcist (1973) - A girl is possessed by the Devil.
  • The Seventh Seal (1957) - A knight challenges Death to a game.
  • O Auto da Compadecida (2000) - Two peasants are judged.
  • The Invention of Lying (2009) - Someone invents an idea.
  • The Matrix (1999) - A person believes he is Jesus.
  • Blade Runner 2049 (2017) - Officer K struggles with free will under God.
  • Mad Max: Fury Road (2015) - Two people are against an omnipotent desert.
  • City of God (2002) - Buscapé learns goodness.
  • Tree of Life (2011) - Jack is a kid and we are just watching his childhood.
